Research within the Department of Advanced Robotics concentrates on an innovative, multidisciplinary approach to humanoid design and control, and the development of novel robotic components and technologies.


This encompasses activities from both the hard (mechanical/ electrical design and fabrication, sensor systems, actuation development etc.) and soft (control, computer software, human factors etc) systems areas of robotics.
